# YouTube Skippable In-Stream Ad — Trades & Technical Services (DACH)
> PremiumBrain.ai | Duration: 30-45 seconds | Format: Skippable In-Stream

---

## 🎬 Creative Concept: "Der Neue"

**Core Insight:** Every trades business has the same nightmare: the apprentice who almost broke something because nobody told them about the safety exception on page 47.

**Emotional Hook:** The universal "first day on the job" anxiety in a workshop.

---

## 🎨 Key Frame Descriptions

### Key Frame 1 — The Hook
- **Composition:** Medium shot, slightly low angle (making the machine look imposing)
- **Subject:** Young apprentice (~18-20), trades workwear, holding massive spiral-bound manual
- **Setting:** Clean, well-lit workshop (NOT a messy garage — this is a premium business)
- **Props:** Prominent HVAC/heating system, manual with German text visible
- **Mood:** Overwhelm, "where do I even start?"

### Key Frame 2 — The Problem Montage
- **2a:** Wall of colorful Post-it notes — zoom in reveals handwritten SOPs
- **2b:** Phone screen showing WhatsApp group chat, message search visible
- **2c:** Master technician mid-explanation, hand pointing at a component

- **Composition:** Lutfiya holding an iPad/tablet in a workshop setting (she came to THEM)

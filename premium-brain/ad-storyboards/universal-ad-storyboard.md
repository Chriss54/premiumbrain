> PremiumBrain.ai | Duration: 30-45 seconds | Format: Skippable In-Stream

---

## 🎬 Creative Concept: "The Question"

**Core Insight:** Every company has that one person everyone asks. When that person is busy, sick, or quits — everything stops. PremiumBrain IS that person, but it never sleeps and speaks every language.

**Emotional Hook:** The universal moment of "I need an answer but nobody is here to help."

---

## 🎨 Key Frame Descriptions

### Key Frame 1 — Four Quadrants
- **Top-left:** Hotel receptionist looking around empty lobby
- **Top-right:** Young technician in workshop looking at machine
- **Bottom-left:** Car dealership salesperson checking empty back office
- **Bottom-right:** Law office paralegal with a stack of case files
- **Style:** Each shot slightly desaturated, same neutral color grading
- **All four:** same body language (slight confusion, looking for help)

### Key Frame 2 — The Stat
- **Full screen black (#0A0A0A)**
- **White text, centered, serif font for gravitas**
- **Text:** *"Companies lose 42% of institutional knowledge with every departure."*
- **Animation:** Text fades in word by word (typewriter effect, premium speed)

### Key Frame 3 — Logo Reveal
- Same brand animation — red stripe, then text (consistent across all 3 ads)

### Key Frame 4 — Lutfiya Product Demo
- **Composition:** Lutfiya seated, large monitor behind her
- **She speaks directly to camera** — warm, professional, Dr. title in lower third
- **Screen behind her:** Shows REAL product interface with data flowing in
- **Split moment:** when the French question appears, Lutfiya smiles knowingly

### Key Frame 5 — Testimonials
- **Dark cards on dark background** (matching PremiumBrain UI)
- **Each card:** Avatar circle + quote + title
- **Style:** NOT generic stock photo testimonials — styled like real product reviews
- **Animation:** Cards slide in one by one (0.3s delay between each)

### Key Frame 6 — End Card
- Same brand template — dark bg, red glow, logo, CTA
- Consistent across all 3 ad creatives

## 📊 Cross-Campaign Consistency Guide

All three ads (Hotel, Trades, Universal) share:
- **Same logo animation** (red stripe → PremiumBrain text)
- **Same end card template** (dark bg, red glow, CTA button)
- **Same audio branding** (tech activation sound at logo reveal)
- **Same Chris → Lutfiya → Chris dynamic** (problem → solution → CTA)
- **Same color grading:** Slightly desaturated for problem shots, clean/bright for solution, premium dark for CTAs
- **Same lower third style** for Lutfiya: "Dr. Lutfiya Miller — Co-Founder"
- **Same CTA:** Always points to PremiumBrain.ai landing page (never YouTube channel)

This ensures brand recognition across campaigns. When someone sees the second and third ad, the red stripe + sound = instant recognition.
